Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. This application respects your privacy and does not collect or store your personal data online. All data generated by the application is stored locally on your device and is used solely for the app's internal functionality.

Data Stored Locally

All data used in this application is stored exclusively on your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Results and calculation history
  • Usage statistics related to training and performance

Data Export and Import Functionality

An option to export and import data may be available. This allows you to transfer your data between devices. Exported data remains under your control, and no data exported from the app is shared with us or any third parties.

Advertisements

While the application is designed to work offline, certain features, such as advertisements, may require an internet connection. We work with third-party ad networks to display ads within the app. These networks may collect some anonymized information, but no information from the app itself is shared.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated to comply with applicable laws or to enhance your privacy protection. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any updates.
